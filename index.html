Connecting Boaters with Marine Services

Our Product

What is it?

Mariner Exchange is a mobile app that connects boaters with marine services. On Mariner Exchange, you can find ratings and reviews on marine service providers and pay over the platform.

How it Works

1. Identify your service needs

Select what services you need and where you need them.

2. Select a service provider

Read crowd sourced ratings and reviews on top marine service providers who have the specialty you need. Contact the one that's right for you.

3. Pay seamlessly

Mariner Exchange features an optional payment system. After you arrange service, you can receive estimates from the service provider and pay seamlessly over the platform.
